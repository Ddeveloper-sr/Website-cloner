How this works: everything runs in your browser tab — there's no backend. Cross-site fetches are routed through a public CORS proxy; use Test to check one is alive before a full crawl. The crawler auto-detects rate-limiting and slows down for the rest of a run.

Limits: up to 60 assets and 80MB total per clone. Individual files over 8MB are skipped.

What to expect: static HTML/CSS/images/fonts clone well. React/Vue/Next.js sites often render mostly-empty HTML in the raw response since their content is built by JavaScript after load — this tool fetches HTML but doesn't execute JS, so those sites will look incomplete. Login-gated and anti-bot-protected sites will also block this.
